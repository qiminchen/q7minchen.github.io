I am a Master's student from UC San Diego studying Computational Science, advised by Prof. David J. Kriegman and Prof. Zhuowen Tu. I received my M.S. degree from UCSD, and B.S degree from Fuzhou University.

My research interest mainly focuses on Computer Vision, especially generative model for 3D shape and scene understanding and reconstruction. I am also interested in object detection and segmentation.
