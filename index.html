Research

My research interest mainly focuses on Computer Vision, especially generative model for 3D shape and scene understanding and reconstruction. I am also interested in object detection and segmentation.

  1. CoralNet Beta: Deploy Deep Neural Networks for Coral Reef Analysis

    • Developed core deep learning codebase for VGG, ResNet and EfficientNet efficient training and testing pipelines in Pytorch.
    • Worked on CoralNet deep learning backend development.
    • Evaluated image features extracted from CoralNet using Logistic Regression, Multi-Layer Perceptron, SVM, Random Forest and Gaussian Naive Bayes.
  2. Topology-Aware Single-Image 3D Shape Reconstruction
  3. Detecting Line Segments as Objects
